WORLD OF WARCRAFT: MIDNIGHT
INSCRIPTION LEVELING GUIDE (1–100)
========================================
Last Updated: January 27, 2026

GUIDE GOAL
----------
This guide is focused on the cheapest possible route to max Inscription skill (100), while also
including all 29 first-time craft rewards for maximum bonus experience.

General Tip: Always buy the cheapest quality of materials available. Quality does not affect
the outcome when leveling a profession.


MATERIALS REQUIRED (SKILL 1–100)
----------------------------------
- Sanguithorn Pigment: 50
- Mana Lily Pigment: 35
- Thalassian Songwater: 100
- Argentleaf Pigment: 20
- Soul Cipher: 58
- Sienna Ink: 53
- Munsell Ink: 43
- Tranquility Bloom: 140 (used for milling)
- Azeroot: 85
- Mote of Pure Void: 15
- Mote of Light: 4
- Mote of Primal Energy: 7
- Mote of Wild Magic: 13
- Duskshrouded Stone: 1

Note: Depending on your luck with crafts, you may need to purchase slightly more materials than listed.


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 1 TO 50
------------------------------
Follow these crafts in order to level efficiently.

Skill 1–10: Mill Tranquility Bloom
Skill 10–11: Craft Sienna Ink
Skill 11–15: Mill Tranquility Bloom
Skill 15–21: Craft Munsell Ink
Skill 21–22: Craft Faunatender's Baton
Skill 22–23: Craft Floratender's Crutch
Skill 23–24: Craft Rootwarden's Lamp
Skill 24–25: Craft Soul Cipher
Skill 25–26: Craft Codified Azeroot
Skill 26–28: Craft Hobbyist Alchemist's Mixing Rod
Skill 28–31: Craft Hobbyist Rolling Pin
Skill 31–33: Craft Hobbyist Scribe's Quill
Skill 33–34: Craft Thalassian Missive of the Aurora
Skill 34–35: Craft Thalassian Missive of the Feverflare
Skill 35–36: Craft Faunatender's Trust
Skill 36–37: Craft Thalassian Missive of the Fireflash
Skill 37–38: Craft Thalassian Missive of the Harmonious
Skill 38–40: Craft Hobbyist Rolling Pin
Skill 40–45: Craft Thalassian Missive of the Harmonious
Skill 45–46: Craft Thalassian Missive of the Quickblade
Skill 46–50: Craft Thalassian Missive of the Peerless


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 50 TO 65 — DISCOVERY PHASE
--------------------------------------------------
Important: This section is discovery-based and works differently from the rest of the guide.

You will be crafting Treatises repeatedly. Each time you craft one, you have a chance to
discover a new recipe. Continue crafting Treatises until you have discovered all available
recipes and crafted each one at least once to claim every first-time craft bonus.

The number of crafts required will vary depending on your luck with discoveries.

Skill 50–65: Craft Treatises (repeat until all first-craft bonus recipes are discovered and completed)


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 65 TO 100
--------------------------------
Skill 65–100: Craft Vantus Rune: Radiant


COMPLETION NOTES
----------------
Once you reach skill 100, you have maxed out Inscription in World of Warcraft: Midnight.
Any extra crafted items can be sold on the Auction House to recover some of the gold spent on materials.
