WORLD OF WARCRAFT: MIDNIGHT
BLACKSMITHING LEVELING GUIDE (1–100)
========================================

MATERIALS REQUIRED
------------------
- Duskshrouded Stone: 3
- Luminant Flux: 255

Note: Depending on your luck with crafts, you may need to purchase slightly more materials than listed.


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 1 TO 50
------------------------------
Follow these crafts in order to level efficiently.

Skill 1–10: Craft Refulgent Copper Ingot
Skill 10–12: Craft Refulgent Repair Hammer
Skill 12–13: Craft Thalassian Skinning Knife
Skill 13–14: Craft Thalassian Sickle
Skill 14–15: Craft Thalassian Pickaxe
Skill 15–18: Craft Blood-Tempered Greaves
Skill 18–20: Craft Blood-Tempered Chestplate
Skill 20–21: Craft Thalassian Leatherworker's Knife
Skill 21–22: Craft Gloaming Alloy
Skill 22–24: Craft Blood-Tempered Bracers
Skill 24–26: Craft Blood-Tempered Greatbelt
Skill 26–27: Craft Thalassian Blacksmith's Toolbox — Equip it if you don't already have one.
Skill 27–28: Craft Thalassian Blacksmith's Hammer — Equip it if you don't already have one.
Skill 28–30: Craft Dawnforged Edge
Skill 30–31: Craft Dawnforged Ritual Knife
Skill 31–32: Craft Primalforged Heavy Axe
Skill 32–34: Craft Blood-Tempered Basinet
Skill 34–36: Craft Blood-Tempered Bulwark
Skill 36–37: Craft Dawnforged Long Blade
Skill 37–38: Craft Dawnforged Splitter
Skill 38–40: Craft Blood-Tempered Gauntlets
Skill 40–42: Craft Blood-Tempered Pauldrons
Skill 42–44: Craft Blood-Tempered Leggings
Skill 44–46: Craft Dawnforged War Mace
Skill 46–50: Craft Primalforged Knuckles


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 50 TO 100
--------------------------------
Skill 50–60: Craft Sterling Alloy
Skill 60–100: Craft Thalassian Blacksmith's Hammer


COMPLETION NOTES
----------------
Once you reach skill 100, you have maxed out Blacksmithing in World of Warcraft: Midnight.
Any extra crafted items can be sold on the Auction House to recover some of the gold spent on materials.
