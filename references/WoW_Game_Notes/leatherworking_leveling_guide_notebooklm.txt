WORLD OF WARCRAFT: MIDNIGHT
LEATHERWORKING LEVELING GUIDE (1–100)
========================================
Last Updated: January 26, 2026

GUIDE GOAL
----------
This guide is focused on the cheapest possible route to max Leatherworking skill (100), while also
including all 30 first-time craft rewards for maximum bonus experience.

General Tip: Always buy the cheapest quality of materials available. Quality does not affect
the outcome when leveling a profession.

LEVELING PATH: SKILL 67 TO 100 — CHOOSE YOUR RECIPE
------------------------------------------------------
Important: To continue past skill 67, you need a new pattern that is not learned automatically.
The two recommended options are Blessed Pango Charm or Primal Spore Binding.
Both recipes can be purchased on the Auction House.

Tip: Use Undermine.Exchange to find a realm where your chosen recipe is cheaply available.
Also compare the material costs of both options before deciding, as prices vary by realm.


OPTION A: Blessed Pango Charm (Skill 67–100)
- Mote of Wild Magic: 350
- Carving Canine: 35
- Peerless Plumage: 35
- Duskshrouded Stone: 70
- Void-Tempered Hide: 35


OPTION B: Primal Spore Binding (Skill 67–100)
- Mote of Primal Energy: 350
- Fantastic Fur: 35
- Scalewoven Hide: 35


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 67 TO 100
--------------------------------
Skill 67–100: Craft your chosen recipe — either Blessed Pango Charm or Primal Spore Binding — repeatedly until you reach skill 100.


COMPLETION NOTES
----------------
Once you reach skill 100, you have maxed out Leatherworking in World of Warcraft: Midnight.
Any extra crafted items can be sold on the Auction House to recover some of the gold spent on materials.
