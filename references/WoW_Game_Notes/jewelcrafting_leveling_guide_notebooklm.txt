WORLD OF WARCRAFT: MIDNIGHT
JEWELCRAFTING LEVELING GUIDE (1–100)
========================================
Last Updated: January 26, 2026

GUIDE GOAL
----------
This guide is focused on the cheapest possible route to max Jewelcrafting skill (100), while also
including all 30 first-time craft rewards for maximum bonus experience.

General Tip: Always buy the cheapest quality of materials available. Quality does not affect
the outcome when leveling a profession.


MATERIALS REQUIRED: SKILL 1 TO 65
------------------------------------
Note: Materials for skill 65–100 are listed separately below, as they depend on which recipe you choose.

- Sin'dorei Lens: 2
- Refulgent Copper Ore: 50
- Crystalline Glass: 105
- Glimmering Gemdust: 50
- Harandar Peridot: 4
- Tenebrous Amethyst: 4
- Amani Lapis: 4

- Flawless Amani Lapis: 2
- Flawless Harandar Peridot: 2
- Flawless Tenebrous Amethyst: 2
- Flawless Sanguine Garnet: 2
- Umbral Tin Ore: 5

Note: Depending on your luck with crafts, you may need to purchase slightly more materials than listed.

LEVELING PATH: SKILL 65 TO 100 — CHOOSE YOUR RECIPE
------------------------------------------------------
Important: To continue past skill 65, you need a new pattern that is not learned automatically.
The two recommended options are Prismatic Focusing Iris or Stabilizing Gemstone Bandolier.
Both recipes can be purchased on the Auction House.

Tip: Use Undermine.Exchange to find a realm where your chosen recipe is cheaply available.
Also compare the material costs of both options before deciding, as prices vary by realm.


OPTION A: Prismatic Focusing Iris (Skill 65–100)
- Sin'dorei Lens: 80
- Glimmering Gemdust: 80


OPTION B: Stabilizing Gemstone Bandolier (Skill 65–100)
- Sanguine Garnet: 40
- Harandar Peridot: 40
- Tenebrous Amethyst: 40
- Amani Lapis: 40
- Glimmering Gemdust: 120


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 65 TO 100
--------------------------------
Skill 65–100: Craft your chosen recipe — either Prismatic Focusing Iris or Stabilizing Gemstone Bandolier — repeatedly until you reach skill 100.


COMPLETION NOTES
----------------
Once you reach skill 100, you have maxed out Jewelcrafting in World of Warcraft: Midnight.
Any extra crafted items can be sold on the Auction House to recover some of the gold spent on materials.
