WORLD OF WARCRAFT: MIDNIGHT
INSCRIPTION GUIDE: RECIPES & KNOWLEDGE POINTS
========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
This guide covers all of Midnight Inscription, including every weekly and one-time Knowledge
Point source, profession equipment, specialization trees, renown-gated recipes, and full recipe
lists for weapons, Darkmoon cards, contracts, treatises, missives, reagents, and housing decor.

For leveling Inscription from 1 to 100, a separate leveling guide is available.
For specialization builds and recommended Knowledge Point allocation, a separate specialization
guide is available.


WHAT CHANGED FROM THE WAR WITHIN
----------------------------------
Artisan Scribe's Moxie: The shared Artisan's Acuity currency from The War Within has been
replaced by a profession-specific currency called Artisan Scribe's Moxie, which can only be
spent on Inscription-specific recipes and items.

No More Bronze Quality Reagents: Bronze quality has been removed. All reagents and consumables
now only have Silver and Gold quality levels, meaning you always receive at least Silver quality.
Concentration can be used immediately to target Gold quality. Weapons, profession equipment,
and Darkmoon trinkets still have 5 ranks.

Epic Profession Tools: A new Epic quality tier of profession tools is available with higher
secondary stats than Rare tools. Both Epic and Rare tools provide the same base Skill bonus.

Bows: Inscription can now craft bows, giving Hunters an alternative to Engineering for crafted
ranged weapons. Engineering was previously the only profession able to craft them.

Darkmoon Card Crafting Returns: In The War Within, Darkmoon cards only dropped from mobs. In
Midnight, crafting is back. Scribes create individual cards through the new Darkmoon Curiosity
specialization, collect all 8 cards of a suit into a deck, and craft a Darkmoon Dominion
trinket (item level 220, 5 ranks) from the completed deck. Each suit also has a matching
Darkmoon Sigil embellishment.


WEEKLY KNOWLEDGE POINT SOURCES
--------------------------------
Midnight provides approximately 20 to 25 Knowledge Points per week from four sources. The
actual total depends on whether you can meet the quality requirements for Patron Orders.

Patron Crafting Orders (approximately 24 KP per week): Patron orders are the primary weekly
Knowledge Point source. Multiple orders are available throughout the week, but not all of them
award Knowledge Points. Those that do reward a Glimmer of Midnight Inscription Knowledge.

Weekly Quest (2 KP): Your profession trainer offers a weekly quest requiring you to complete
3 Crafting Orders. Completing it rewards a Thalassian Scribe's Journal worth 2 Knowledge
Points. This quest resets weekly.

Weekly Drops (4 KP): Each week, one of each of the following items can be looted from
treasures in the new Midnight zones: Brilliant Phoenix Ink and Loa-Blessed Rune. Each is
worth 2 KP.

Inscription Treatise (1 to 2 KP): Scribes can use a Thalassian Treatise on Inscription weekly
for 1 Knowledge Point. Treatises are Bind on Pickup but Warbound, so you can craft extras and
send them to alts. If you fully unlock the Calm Hands specialization, your weekly treatise
grants an additional Knowledge Point for a total of 2 KP.

Catch-Up System: If you fall behind or start late, Patron Orders will reward Flickers of
Midnight Inscription Knowledge instead of Glimmers. Flickers provide more Knowledge Points
per item. Once you have caught up, orders will return to giving Glimmers.

INSCRIPTION SPECIALIZATIONS
-----------------------------
Four specialization trees unlock progressively as you level Inscription, becoming available at
skill levels 25, 50, 60, and 75.

Blueprints: Covers tools, staves, off-hands, and bows. Includes sub-specializations for Staves,
Off-Hands, and Bows. The Bows sub-specialization is new in Midnight.

Calm Hands: Covers treatises and knowledge items. Fully unlocking this tree grants an extra
Knowledge Point from your weekly Inscription treatise.

Darkmoon Curiosity: Covers Darkmoon cards, decks, sigils, and the Darkmoon Dominion trinkets.
This is a new dedicated tree in Midnight; in The War Within, Darkmoon cards were grouped with
missives and contracts.

Perfected Products: Covers crafting stat bonuses including Multicraft, Resourcefulness, and
Ingenuity. This tree is smaller and cheaper to max than in The War Within, so capping your
crafting stats requires less investment.


PROFESSION EQUIPMENT
---------------------
Epic profession tools are new in Midnight. Epic tools have higher secondary stats than Rare
tools but provide the same Skill bonus.

To fully equip a Scribe, items from two professions are needed. The Quill (tool) is crafted
by Inscription itself. Both accessories (Spectacles and Magnifying Glass) come from Jewelcrafting.

Tool — Inscription:
- Green: Hobbyist Scribe's Quill (learned from trainer)
- Blue (Rare): Sin'dorei Quill (from Blueprints specialization)
- Epic: Gilded Sin'dorei Quill (recipe sold by Lelorian in Silvermoon City for 150 Artisan Scribe's Moxie)

Accessory — Spectacles (Jewelcrafting):
- Green: Bold Biographer's Bifocals
- Blue: Sin'dorei Scribe's Spectacles
- Epic: Flawless Text Scrutinizers

Accessory — Magnifying Glass (Jewelcrafting):
- Green: Fantastic Font Focuser
- Blue: Improved Right-Handed Magnifying Glass
- Epic: Thalassian Scribe's Crystalline Lens

Tool Tip: Consider keeping two tools — one with Resourcefulness for milling (which does not
benefit from Multicraft) and one with Multicraft for crafting inks, reagents, missives, and
everything else. Swap between them depending on what you are doing.

DARKMOON CARDS, DECKS, AND TRINKETS
--------------------------------------
Darkmoon cards are one of Inscription's signature products. In Midnight, crafting returns
after being absent in The War Within, where cards only dropped from mobs.

How the system works: There are four suits — Blood, Hunt, Rot, and Void. Each suit contains
8 cards. To obtain a trinket, you craft or collect all 8 cards of one suit, combine them into
a deck, and then have a Scribe craft the deck into a Darkmoon Dominion trinket. The trinket
is item level 220 with 5 ranks.

Note: Both cards and Thalassian Essence of the Faire also drop from world content for all
players, not just Scribes. Anyone can collect cards and combine them into a deck, which
functions as a lower item level trinket on its own.

Inscribe Recipes (Darkmoon Curiosity specialization): Each suit has an Inscribe recipe that
creates a random card from that suit. Reagents accepted are Munsell Ink, Sienna Ink, or
Thalassian Essence of the Faire.

Transcribe Recipes (Darkmoon Curiosity specialization): Each suit has a Transcribe recipe
that swaps an existing card for a different random card from the same suit. This costs a
Thalassian Essence of the Faire and is useful for targeting a specific missing card.

Darkmoon Dominion Trinkets (all from Darkmoon Curiosity specialization):
- Darkmoon Dominion: Blood
- Darkmoon Dominion: Hunt
- Darkmoon Dominion: Rot
- Darkmoon Dominion: Void

Darkmoon Sigil Embellishments (all from Darkmoon Curiosity specialization):
Darkmoon Sigils are optional reagents that add embellishment effects to crafted gear. Each
requires a full completed deck to craft. You can wear up to 2 embellished items at a time.
- Darkmoon Sigil: Blood
- Darkmoon Sigil: Hunt
- Darkmoon Sigil: Rot
- Darkmoon Sigil: Void
