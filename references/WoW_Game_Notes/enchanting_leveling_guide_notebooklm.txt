WORLD OF WARCRAFT: MIDNIGHT
ENCHANTING LEVELING GUIDE (1–100)
========================================
Last Updated: January 26, 2026

GUIDE GOAL
----------
This guide is focused on the cheapest possible route to max Enchanting skill (100), while also
including all 18 first-time craft rewards for maximum bonus experience.

General Tip: Always buy the cheapest quality of materials available. Quality does not affect
the outcome when leveling a profession.

Bonus Tip: If you have any excess gear or items lying around, you can disenchant them to gain
skill for free up to around skill 25.


MATERIALS REQUIRED
-------------------
- Refulgent Copper Rod: 1
- Eversinging Dust: 652
- Radiant Shard: 171
- Dawn Crystal: 30
- Sunglass Vial: 1
- Mote of Light: 11
- Mote of Wild Magic: 10
- Flawless Amani Lapis: 1
- Arcanoweave Bolt: 2
- Majestic Fin: 1
- Mote of Primal Energy: 60
- Flawless Harandar Peridot: 12

Note: Depending on your luck with crafts, you may need to purchase slightly more materials than listed.


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 1 TO 62
------------------------------
Follow these crafts in order to level efficiently.

Skill 1–2: Craft Runed Refulgent Copper Rod — Equip it if you don't already have one.
Skill 2–5: Craft Enchant Ring - Thalassian Haste
Skill 5–19: Craft Enchant Ring - Thalassian Versatility
Skill 19–20: Craft Enchant Shoulders - Nature's Grace
Skill 20–23: Craft Enchant Helm - Rune of Avoidance
Skill 23–24: Craft Thalassian Phoenix Oil
Skill 24–26: Craft Enchant Weapon - Berserker's Rage
Skill 26–27: Craft Illusory Adornment - Blooming Light
Skill 27–39: Craft Enchant Ring - Nature's Wrath
Skill 39–40: Craft Enchant Shoulders - Flight of the Eagle
Skill 40–42: Craft Enchant Ring - Nature's Wrath
Skill 42–43: Craft Enchant Helm - Hex of Leeching
Skill 43–45: Craft Enchant Weapon - Arcane Mastery
Skill 45–49: Craft Enchant Ring - Amani Mastery
Skill 49–52: Craft Thalassian Spellweaver's Wand
Skill 52–58: Craft Enchant Ring - Amani Mastery
Skill 58–59: Craft Enchant Shoulders - Thalassian Recovery
Skill 59–60: Craft Enchant Helm - Blessing of Speed
Skill 60–62: Craft Enchant Ring - Amani Mastery


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 62 TO 67 — HIGHER COST PHASE
----------------------------------------------------
Note: From skill 62 onward, materials become more expensive. Plan your purchases accordingly.

Skill 62–64: Craft Enchant Weapon - Jan'alai's Precision
Skill 64–67: Craft Enchant Weapon - Worldsoul Aegis


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 67 TO 100
--------------------------------
Tip: Depending on your specialization choices, you may be able to find alternative routes to
skill 100. The path below is the general recommended route.

Skill 67–100: Craft Enchant Weapon - Worldsoul Aegis repeatedly until you reach skill 100.


COMPLETION NOTES
----------------
Once you reach skill 100, you have maxed out Enchanting in World of Warcraft: Midnight.
Enchants crafted during leveling can be sold on the Auction House to recover some of the gold
spent on materials.
