WORLD OF WARCRAFT: MIDNIGHT
BLACKSMITHING GUIDE: RECIPES & KNOWLEDGE POINTS
========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
This guide covers all of Midnight Blacksmithing, including every weekly and one-time Knowledge
Point source, profession equipment, and full recipe lists for plate armor, weapons,
embellishments, alloys, and housing decor.

For leveling Blacksmithing from 1 to 100, a separate leveling guide is available.
For specialization builds and recommended Knowledge Point allocation, a separate specialization
guide is available.


WHAT CHANGED FROM THE WAR WITHIN
----------------------------------
Quality Tiers Reduced: Bronze quality reagents and consumables have been removed. All reagents
and consumables now only have Silver and Gold quality levels, meaning you always receive at
least Silver quality. Concentration can be used immediately to target Gold quality. Weapons,
armor, and profession equipment still have 5 ranks.

Artisan Blacksmith's Moxie: The shared Artisan's Acuity currency from The War Within has been
replaced by a profession-specific currency called Artisan Blacksmith's Moxie, which can only
be spent on Blacksmithing-specific recipes and items.

Epic Profession Tools: A new Epic quality tier of profession tools is available with higher
secondary stats than Rare tools. Both Epic and Rare tools provide the same Skill bonus.

Ignition Buff Removed: The temporary crafting buff from The War Within, which required crafting
a special ignition item before crafting gear, has been removed. Permanent stat bonuses are now
granted through the specialization tree instead.

Frameworks Removed: The framework optional reagent system from The War Within has been removed.
These optional reagents now come from Patron Order bag rewards instead.


WEEKLY KNOWLEDGE POINT SOURCES
--------------------------------
Midnight provides approximately 31 Knowledge Points per week from four sources. The actual
total depends on whether you can meet the quality requirements for Patron Orders.

Patron Crafting Orders (approximately 24 KP per week): Patron orders are the primary weekly
Knowledge Point source. Multiple orders are available throughout the week, but not all of them
award Knowledge Points. Those that do reward a Glimmer of Midnight Blacksmithing Knowledge.

Weekly Quest (2 KP): Your profession trainer offers a weekly quest requiring you to complete
3 Crafting Orders. Completing it rewards a Thalassian Blacksmith's Journal worth 2 Knowledge
Points. This quest resets weekly.

Weekly Drops (4 KP): Each week, one of each of the following items can be looted from
treasures in the new Midnight zones: Thalassian Whetstone and Infused Quenching Oil.

Inscription Treatise (1 KP): A Thalassian Treatise on Blacksmithing provides 1 Knowledge
Point. Treatises are Bind on Pickup but Warbound, so an alt with Inscription can craft one
and mail it to your main. Submit a Public Crafting Order to obtain one if you do not have an
Inscription alt.

Catch-Up System: If you fall behind or start late, Patron Orders will reward Flickers of
Midnight Blacksmithing Knowledge instead of Glimmers until you have caught up.


ONE-TIME KNOWLEDGE POINT SOURCES
----------------------------------
These sources can only be collected once per character. Collect them as early as possible to
accelerate your specialization progress.

Renown Knowledge Book: "Beyond the Event Horizon: Blacksmithing" is sold by the Voidstorm
Renown Quartermaster and provides a one-time Knowledge Point reward.

Blacksmithing Treasures: There are 8 Blacksmithing treasures in Midnight, each providing
3 Knowledge Points for a total of 24 KP available from treasures.

Treasure list:
- Sin'dorei Master's Forgemace — Silvermoon City
- Silvermoon Blacksmith's Hammer — Silvermoon City
- Deconstructed Forge Techniques — Silvermoon City
- Metalworking Cheat Sheet — Eversong Woods
- Silvermoon Smithing Kit — Eversong Woods
- Carefully Racked Spear — Zul'Aman, Atal'Aman
- Rutaani Floratender's Sword — Harandar
- Voidstorm Defense Spear — Voidstorm


BLACKSMITHING SPECIALIZATIONS
-------------------------------
Four specialization trees unlock progressively as you level Blacksmithing, becoming available
at skill levels 25, 50, 60, and 75.


PROFESSION EQUIPMENT
---------------------
Epic profession tools are new in Midnight. Epic tools have higher secondary stats than Rare
tools but provide the same Skill bonus.

Blacksmiths can craft their own Hammer (tool) and Toolbox (accessory). The apron (second
accessory) must be obtained from a Leatherworker.

Tool — Blacksmithing:
- Green: Thalassian Blacksmith's Hammer
- Blue (Rare): Sun-Blessed Blacksmith's Hammer
- Epic: Sunforged Blacksmith's Hammer

Accessory — Toolbox (Blacksmithing):
- Green: Thalassian Blacksmith's Toolbox

- Epic: Sunforged Blacksmith's Toolbox

Accessory — Apron (Leatherworking):
- Green: Apprentice Smith's Apron
- Blue: Sin'dorei Forgemaster's Cover
- Epic: Thalassian Ironbender's Regalia

PLATE ARMOR — FULL RECIPE LIST
--------------------------------
Blacksmiths can craft three tiers of plate armor: Rare (Blue), Epic (Purple), and PvP gear.

Rare armor is learned from the trainer and is well suited for gearing up alts or fresh
characters. Epic armor requires specialization points in Armorsmithing and can be upgraded
to Mythic-raid item levels using the appropriate Crests. PvP gear provides a cheaper
entry point for PvP-focused players, with recipes being Green quality but items scaling
based on Skill and optional reagents.

Rare Plate Armor (all learned from Trainer):
- Blood-Tempered Greaves — Trainer, skill 5.
- Blood-Tempered Chestplate — Trainer, skill 10.
- Blood-Tempered Bracers — Trainer, skill 20.
- Blood-Tempered Greatbelt — Trainer, skill 20.
- Blood-Tempered Basinet — Trainer, skill 25.
- Blood-Tempered Bulwark — Trainer, skill 25.
- Blood-Tempered Gauntlets — Trainer, skill 30.
- Blood-Tempered Pauldrons — Trainer, skill 30.
- Blood-Tempered Leggings — Trainer, skill 35.


WEAPONS — FULL RECIPE LIST
----------------------------
Blacksmiths can craft daggers, swords, axes, maces, fist weapons, polearms, and warglaives.
Epic weapons require points in the Weaponsmithing specialization. Some recipes drop from
specific rare enemies or are sold by vendors in Silvermoon City.

EMBELLISHMENTS
---------------
Embellished gear carries powerful unique effects. You can only equip two embellished items
at a time across all your gear, so choose carefully based on your specialization and playstyle.

Unlike The War Within where some embellishments were unlocked through specializations, all
Midnight Blacksmithing embellishments are world drops. This means you can focus specialization
points on crafting quality rather than recipe unlocks.

- Knight-Commander's Palisade: Chance on hit to gain an absorption shield.
  Source: Drop from Plans (world drop).

- Murder Row Fleet Feet: Chance to increase Avoidance.
  Source: Drop from Lithiel Cinderfury in Murder Row.

- Murder Row Fishhook: Chance to bleed the target for Physical damage.
  Source: Drop from Lithiel Cinderfury in Murder Row.


REAGENTS, ALLOYS, AND MISCELLANEOUS
--------------------------------------
Blacksmiths produce alloys used as required materials in most Blacksmithing recipes, temporary
weapon enhancement buffs (Weaponstones), and utility items like skeleton keys.

Alloys are core crafting inputs. If you plan to craft large quantities, consider investing in
The Old Ways specialization for more efficient alloy production. Weaponstones provide 2-hour
weapon buffs and have consistent Auction House demand.

- Refulgent Copper Ingot (Basic crafting alloy) — Trainer, skill 1.
- Refulgent Repair Hammer (Repairs weapons and plate armor, consumed on use) — Trainer, skill 5.
- Gloaming Alloy (Mid-tier crafting alloy) — Trainer, skill 15.
- Sterling Alloy (Mid-tier crafting alloy) — Trainer, skill 25.
- Refulgent Weightstone (+Attack Power for blunt weapons, 2 hours) — Trainer, skill 40.
- Refulgent Razorstone (+Finesse for gathering tools, 2 hours) — Trainer, skill 45.
- Refulgent Whetstone (+Attack Power for bladed weapons, 2 hours) — Specialization: Weaponsmithing, Weaponstones branch.
- Thalassian Skeleton Key (Opens locked chests, consumed on use) — Vendor: Eriden in Silvermoon City.
