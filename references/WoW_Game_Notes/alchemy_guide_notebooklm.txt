WORLD OF WARCRAFT: MIDNIGHT
ALCHEMY GUIDE: RECIPES & KNOWLEDGE POINTS
========================================

OVERVIEW
--------
This guide covers all of Midnight Alchemy, including every weekly and one-time Knowledge Point
source, profession equipment, Camberon's Cauldron recipe discovery, renown-gated recipes, and
full recipe lists for potions, flasks, phials, cauldrons, transmutations, alchemist stones,
and housing decor.

For leveling Alchemy from 1 to 100, a separate leveling guide is available.
For specialization builds and recommended Knowledge Point allocation, a separate specialization
guide is available.


WHAT CHANGED FROM THE WAR WITHIN
----------------------------------
Midnight Alchemy changed significantly compared to The War Within.

Quality Tiers Reduced: Consumables and reagents now only have two quality levels, Silver and
Gold, instead of three. Flasks and potions craft at a minimum of Silver quality.

Artisan Alchemist's Moxie: The shared Artisan's Acuity currency from The War Within has been
replaced by a profession-specific currency called Artisan Alchemist's Moxie, which can only
be spent on Alchemy-specific recipes and items.

Epic Profession Tools: A new Epic quality tier of profession tools is available, providing more
secondary stats than Rare tools while giving the same Skill bonus.

Camberon's Cauldron: A new deterministic recipe discovery system replaces RNG-based discovery.
Recipes are unlocked by meeting specific crafting requirements, with no random chance involved.

Simplified Specialization Trees: The War Within had five separate sub-trees inside Alchemical
Mastery for each herb type. These are gone. Alchemical Mastery is now a straightforward
efficiency tree with Resourcefulness branches for flasks, potions, and reagents. The
transmutagen minigame involving Gleaming, Ominous, Volatile, and Mercurial transmutagens has
also been removed. Transmutations are now simple material conversions and mote cycling.


WEEKLY KNOWLEDGE POINT SOURCES
--------------------------------
Midnight has four weekly sources of profession Knowledge, providing approximately 23 Knowledge
Points per week in total. Note that Patron Orders require crafting specific items at high
quality, so your actual weekly total may be lower if you cannot meet the quality requirement
for a given order.

Patron Crafting Orders (approximately 16 KP per week): Patron orders are the primary source
of weekly Knowledge Points. Multiple orders are available each week, but not all of them award
Knowledge Points. Those that do reward a Glimmer of Midnight Alchemy Knowledge.

Weekly Quest (2 KP): Your profession trainer offers a weekly quest requiring you to complete
3 Crafting Orders. Completing it rewards a Thalassian Alchemist's Notebook worth 2 Knowledge
Points. This quest resets each week.

Weekly Drops (4 KP): Each week, one of each of the following items can be looted from
treasures scattered across the new Midnight zones: Lightbloomed Spore Sample and Aged Cruor.
Each is worth 2 KP.

Inscription Treatise (1 KP): A Thalassian Treatise on Alchemy provides 1 Knowledge Point.
Treatises are Bind on Pickup, so you must submit a Public Crafting Order to obtain one. They
are Warbound, so an alt with Inscription can craft one and mail it to your main.

Catch-Up System: If you fall behind or start leveling Alchemy late, Patron Orders will reward
Flickers of Midnight Alchemy Knowledge instead of Glimmers. Flickers provide more Knowledge
Points per item, allowing late starters to catch up to players who began earlier.


ONE-TIME KNOWLEDGE POINT SOURCES
----------------------------------
These sources can only be collected once per character.

Renown Knowledge Book: "Beyond the Event Horizon: Alchemy" is a one-time Knowledge Point book
sold by the Voidstorm Renown Quartermaster. It provides 10 Midnight Alchemy Knowledge Points.

Alchemy Treasures: There are 8 Alchemy treasures scattered across Silvermoon City, Zul'Aman,
Harandar, and Voidstorm. Each treasure provides 3 Knowledge Points, for a total of 24 KP
available from treasures. These are one-time pickups per character.

Treasure list:
- Pristine Potion — Silvermoon City
- Freshly Plucked Peacebloom — Silvermoon City
- Vial of Zul'Aman Oddities — Zul'Aman
- Measured Ladle — Atal'Aman
- Vial of Rootlands Oddities — Harandar
- Vial of Voidstorm Oddities — Slayer's Rise
- Failed Experiment — Location not yet confirmed
- Vial of Eversong Oddities — Location not yet confirmed


CAMBERON'S CAULDRON — RECIPE DISCOVERY SYSTEM
-----------------------------------------------
Camberon's Cauldron is a deterministic recipe discovery system located in Silvermoon City,
next to the Alchemy trainer. Unlike the discovery system in The War Within, there is no random
chance involved. Each recipe has a specific crafting requirement that must be met before the
recipe becomes available to research. Some recipes also cost Artisan Alchemist's Moxie.

All Camberon's Cauldron Recipes:

Equipment:
- Primal Philosopher's Stone — Unlock: Level Alchemy to 10. No Moxie cost.

Flasks and Phials:
- Flask of the Blood Knights — Unlock: Drink a Flask of Thalassian Resistance. Cost: 50 Moxie.
- Flask of the Magisters — Unlock: Craft Sin'dorei flasks 10 times. Cost: 50 Moxie.
- Flask of the Shattered Sun — Unlock: Craft Sin'dorei flasks 10 times. Cost: 50 Moxie.
- Haranir Phial of Ingenuity — Unlock: Craft Haranir phials 15 times. Cost: 25 Moxie.

Potions:
- Draught of Rampant Abandon — Unlock: Craft Void Potions 10 times. Cost: 50 Moxie.
- Light's Potential — Unlock: Create 5 Midnight Healing Potions. Cost: 50 Moxie.
- Lightfused Mana Potion — Unlock: Recycle Midnight Potions 5 times. No Moxie cost.
- Potion of Devoured Dreams — Unlock: Craft Void Potions 15 times. Cost: 25 Moxie.
- Potion of Zealotry — Unlock: Brew 10 Light Potions. Cost: 50 Moxie.

Transmutations:
- Bouquet of Herbs — Unlock: Perform 5 Midnight Transmutations. Cost: 25 Moxie.
- Transmute: Mote of Light — Unlock: Perform 5 Midnight Transmutations. Cost: 25 Moxie.
- Transmute: Mote of Primal Energy — Unlock: Perform 5 Midnight Transmutations. Cost: 25 Moxie.
- Transmute: Mote of Pure Void — Unlock: Perform 10 Midnight Transmutations. Cost: 25 Moxie.
- School of Gems — Unlock: Perform 10 Midnight Transmutations. Cost: 25 Moxie.


ALCHEMY SPECIALIZATIONS
------------------------
Four specialization trees are available. They unlock progressively as you level Alchemy,
becoming available at skill levels 25, 50, 60, and 75.


PROFESSION EQUIPMENT
---------------------
Midnight introduces Epic quality profession tools. In The War Within, only Green and Rare
tools existed. Epic tools provide the same Skill bonus as Rare tools but have higher secondary
stats such as Resourcefulness, Multicraft, and Ingenuity.

Alchemists do not craft their own profession equipment. Tools come from Inscription, hats from
Leatherworking, and aprons from Tailoring.

Tool (Inscription):
- Green: Hobbyist Alchemist's Mixing Rod
- Blue (Rare): Sin'dorei Alchemist's Mixing Rod
- Epic: Gilded Alchemist's Mixing Rod

Accessory — Hat (Leatherworking):
- Green: Chemist's Cap
- Blue: Sin'dorei Alchemist's Hat
- Epic: Thalassian Alchemist's Mixcap

Accessory — Apron (Tailoring):
- Green: Bright Linen Alchemy Apron
- Blue: Elegant Artisan's Alchemy Coveralls
- Epic: Thalassian Alchemy Coveralls

----------------------------
Potions are divided into Light and Void categories. Void potions tend to involve drawbacks or
risk-reward tradeoffs. Most potions are unlocked through Camberon's Cauldron.

Light Potions:
- Enlightenment Tonic (Slow fall) — Source: Trainer, skill 5.
- Light's Potential (+Primary Stat) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Light's Preservation (Absorb shield) — Source: Specialization, Potion Prowess tree.
- Lightfused Mana Potion (Restores mana) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Potion of Zealotry (Proc: Holy damage) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Refreshing Serum (Restores health and mana) — Source: Trainer, skill 15.
- Silvermoon Health Potion (Restores health) — Source: Trainer, skill 1.

Void Potions:
- Amani Extract (Heal over time) — Source: Vendor, Magovu in Zul'Aman.
- Draught of Rampant Abandon (+Primary Stat, spawns void zones) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Entropic Extract (Cosmetic appearance change) — Source: Trainer, skill 5.
- Potion of Devoured Dreams (Channel: Restores mana, defenseless during channel) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Potion of Recklessness (+Highest secondary stat, minus lowest secondary stat) — Source: Vendor, Void Researcher Anomander in Voidstorm.
- Void-Shrouded Tincture (Invisibility) — Source: Specialization, Potion Prowess tree.

with Honor.

Flasks:

- Flask of the Blood Knights (+Haste) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Flask of the Magisters (+Mastery) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Flask of the Shattered Sun (+Critical Strike) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.

Haranir Phials:

CAULDRONS
----------
Each cauldron requires 30 points invested in its respective specialization tree, meaning you
must fully commit to either Fluent in Flasks or Potion Prowess to unlock one.

- Cauldron of Sin'dorei Flasks — Source: Specialization, Fluent in Flasks (30 points).
- Voidlight Potion Cauldron — Source: Specialization, Potion Prowess (30 points).


TRANSMUTATIONS — FULL RECIPE LIST
-----------------------------------
Mote transmutations convert 10 of one Mote type into 8 of another. Multicraft can proc,
occasionally yielding more than 8. Most transmutations share an 18-hour cooldown.

Reagent Transmutations (18-hour shared cooldown):
- Bouquet of Herbs (Enchanting materials into herbs) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Box of Rocks (Leather into ore) — Source: Specialization, Transmutation Authority tree.
- School of Gems (Fish into gems) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.

Mote Transmutations (18-hour shared cooldown):
- Transmute: Mote of Light (Primal Energy into Light) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Transmute: Mote of Primal Energy (Pure Void into Primal Energy) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Transmute: Mote of Pure Void (Wild Magic into Pure Void) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
- Transmute: Mote of Wild Magic (Light into Wild Magic) — Source: Trainer, skill 5.

Other Transmutations:
- Composite Flora (Reagent, no cooldown) — Source: Trainer, skill 10.
- Wondrous Synergist (Daily cooldown, 18 hours) — Source: Specialization, Transmutation Authority tree.

Note on Wondrous Synergist: Currently used only in housing decor recipes and both cauldrons.
Its market value is uncertain early in the expansion. Investing heavily into Transmutation
Authority specifically for this recipe is not recommended until the market stabilizes.


ALCHEMIST STONES
-----------------
- Primal Philosopher's Stone (Rare quality, basic stat item) — Source: Camberon's Cauldron.
