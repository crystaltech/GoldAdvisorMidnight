WORLD OF WARCRAFT: MIDNIGHT
TAILORING LEVELING GUIDE (1–100)
========================================
Last Updated: January 26, 2026

GUIDE GOAL
----------
This guide is focused on the cheapest possible route to max Tailoring skill (100), while also
including all 24 first-time craft rewards for maximum bonus experience.

General Tip: Always buy the cheapest quality of materials available. Quality does not affect
the outcome when leveling a profession.


MATERIALS REQUIRED
-------------------
- Bright Linen Bolt: 66
- Imbued Bright Linen Cloth: 33
- Embroidery Floss: 70
- Silverleaf Thread: 78
- Eversinging Dust: 14
- Bright Linen: 1
- Mote of Primal Energy: 6
- Mote of Pure Void: 6
- Sunfire Silk Bolt: 10
- Arcanoweave Bolt: 10
- Sunfire Silk Bolt or Arcanoweave Bolt: 100 (choose one for skill 54–100; see below)
- Sunfire Silk or Arcanoweave Silk: 300 (must match your bolt choice above)

Note: Depending on your luck with crafts, you may need to purchase slightly more materials than listed.


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 1 TO 50
------------------------------
Follow these crafts in order to level efficiently.

Skill 1–2: Craft Bright Linen Bolt
Skill 2–14: Craft Bright Linen Bandage
Skill 14–16: Craft Courtly Wrists
Skill 16–18: Craft Courtly Belt
Skill 18–20: Craft Courtly Slippers
Skill 20–22: Craft Courtly Gloves
Skill 22–24: Craft Courtly Cloak
Skill 24–26: Craft Courtly Pants
Skill 26–28: Craft Courtly Helm
Skill 28–29: Craft Imbued Bright Linen Bolt
Skill 29–31: Craft Bright Linen Alchemy Apron
Skill 31–33: Craft Bright Linen Enchanting Hat
Skill 33–35: Craft Bright Linen Fishing Hat
Skill 35–36: Craft Bright Linen Herbalism Hat
Skill 36–37: Craft Bright Linen Tailoring Robe — Equip it if you don't already have one.
Skill 37–38: Craft Bright Linen Cooking Chapeau
Skill 38–39: Craft Bright Linen Reagent Satchel
Skill 39–40: Craft Imbued Bright Linen Backpack
Skill 40–41: Craft Courtly Robes
Skill 41–42: Craft Bright Linen Spellthread
Skill 42–50: Craft Courtly Shoulders


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 50 TO 54 — HIGHER COST PHASE
----------------------------------------------------
Note: From skill 50 onward, materials become more expensive. Consider selling bags on the
Auction House to recover costs, or equip them yourself to get value out of them.

Skill 50–52: Craft Sunfire Silk Backpack
Skill 52–54: Craft Arcanoweave Reagent Rucksack


LEVELING PATH: SKILL 54 TO 100 — CHOOSE YOUR RECIPE
------------------------------------------------------
Important: From skill 54 onward, leveling becomes significantly harder, especially early in the
Midnight expansion before materials become more widely available. Prices are expected to decrease
over time as more materials enter the market.

To continue, you must purchase one of the following recipes from the Auction House:
- Arcanoweave Lining
- Sunfire Silk Lining

Tip: Check Undermine.Exchange to find which recipe and its materials are cheapest on your realm
or nearby realms. Compare the price of Arcanoweave Bolts versus Sunfire Silk Bolts and choose
whichever complete path is less expensive.

Skill 54–100: Craft either Arcanoweave Lining or Sunfire Silk Lining repeatedly until you reach skill 100.


COMPLETION NOTES
----------------
Once you reach skill 100, you have maxed out Tailoring in World of Warcraft: Midnight.
Any extra crafted items can be sold on the Auction House to recover some of the gold spent on materials.
